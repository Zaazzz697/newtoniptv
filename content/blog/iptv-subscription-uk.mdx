## What Is an IPTV Subscription?

An **IPTV subscription** gives you access to live television channels, movies, and on-demand content delivered via the internet — instead of satellite or cable.

Think of it like Netflix, but instead of just on-demand shows, you also get live TV: sports, news, entertainment, kids' channels — thousands of them, streaming in real time.

For UK viewers, the attraction is simple: all the channels you get from Sky and BT, at a fraction of the price.
