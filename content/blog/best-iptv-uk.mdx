## How to Set Up IPTV in the UK

1. Choose your provider and get your credentials (M3U URL or Xtream Codes)
2. Download an IPTV app (IPTV Smarters, TiviMate, etc.)
3. Enter your credentials in the app
4. Wait for channels to load (typically 1–3 minutes first time)
5. Browse and enjoy

Setup takes under 10 minutes on any device.
